Activity 1

This activity is related to IoT application and its features.

What is an IOT application?

Basically, an IoT application refers to exchanging of information between devices using internet. These devices are embedded with sensors, software and other technologies.

What are the features of an IOT application?

  • Connectivity - ensuring proper connection between devices
  • Analyzation of data - can be used to make effective decisions
  • Sensing- different sensors are required

What are some examples?

  • Home security devices
  • Emergency services
  • Smart wearables- watch
  • Home appliances - TV, Lights etc.
